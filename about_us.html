About Shankar Transport

Reliable lorry & tanker services with pay-as-you-go flexibility

Who We Are

Shankar Transport is a dedicated road transport service provider specializing in lorry services and tanker services. We focus on safe, timely movement of goods and liquids for businesses and individual customers.


Our Services

We offer a wide range of transport solutions:

  • Lorry services for general cargo, materials, and equipment.
  • Tanker services for liquid transport, handled with strict safety standards.
  • Short-distance and long-distance trips based on your requirement.

Pay-as-You-Go Concept

With our pay-as-you-go concept, you only pay for the trips and services you actually use. There are:

  • No long-term lock-in contracts.
  • Transparent pricing for each trip.
  • Flexible scheduling based on your demand.

Why Choose Shankar Transport?

We aim to make transport simple and dependable:

  • Experienced drivers and well-maintained vehicles.
  • Focus on safety, punctuality, and clear communication.
  • Flexible pay-as-you-go model that fits your business cash flow.
